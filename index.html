About Me

Veshu Ghaley

Hello! I'm Veshu, the founder of Veshu Cycling in Thimphu, Bhutan. After experiencing the efficiency and joy of Japanese bike rental systems during my travels, I was inspired to bring this concept to my hometown.

Our mission is to provide high-quality bicycles for rent at affordable prices, making it easy for both locals and tourists to explore Thimphu in an eco-friendly way while promoting healthy living.

With a focus on customer satisfaction and sustainable transportation, Veshu Cycling aims to become the premier bike rental service in Bhutan, combining Japanese efficiency with Bhutanese hospitality.
